Frequently asked questions

1

Who can become an Quidity partner?

We welcome agencies, consultants, creators, VC firms, go-to-market professionals, and individuals who want to share the Quidity love. If you believe in the power of AI-driven sales and are passionate about helping your teams succeed, you’re a great fit.

2

How and when do I get paid?

We use PartnerStack to manage payments securely. Once your commissions are approved, you can track your earnings and withdraw funds anytime via PayPal, Stripe, or direct deposit.

3

Can I be both a customer and a partner?

Absolutely. Many of our strongest partners started as customers and grew with us over time.

4

Do I need a large audience to join?

Not at all. We value quality relationships and genuine alignment far more than audience size.

5

What support will I get as a partner?

You’ll have our partner manager guiding you every step of the way, along with access to resources, training, and co-marketing opportunities.

6

How much have partners earned so far?

Our partner network has earned over $1,000,000 in commissions, and we’re just getting started.
