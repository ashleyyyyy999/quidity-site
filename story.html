Our Story

We built this because it had to exist.

See current openings
🍀

The reason this exists.

Most tools are built to grow. Not to solve. They expand, add features, and become something nobody asked for — but everyone is stuck with.

We started Quidity because we were tired of that. Tired of software that made you feel like you were always one subscription away from getting things done. Tired of dashboards that showed you everything except what actually mattered.

We think software should earn its place. It either solves a real problem — clearly, quietly, every day — or it doesn't deserve to exist. That's the test we hold everything we build to.

We're a small team on purpose. Small means we can care about the details big companies ignore. Small means every person here leaves a mark. Small means we can ship something that actually works instead of something that just demos well.

We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the one you'd miss.

What we actually believe.

Software that earns its place.

It doesn't get to live on your dock because it's popular. It earns it by solving a real problem — quietly, without fanfare — every single day.

Small is a choice, not a limit.

We stay lean deliberately. Smaller teams make sharper decisions. Fewer layers means the people closest to the work are the ones making the calls.

Build it right or don't build it.

The fastest way to slow down is shipping something half-finished. We'd rather miss a trend than ship something we're not proud of.

Real beats impressive.

Things that wow in demos but break in real life are worthless. We care about what a product does for you on day 40, not day 1.

A team worth talking about.

We hire slowly and carefully. Everyone here chose to be here.

Why people choose to stay.

We've thought hard about what makes somewhere worth working at. Here's what we came up with.

Remote-first and always will be.

Ownership stake in what you build.

Health coverage, wherever you are.

Annual team offsite somewhere worth going.

Direct access — no layers, no politics.

Annual learning budget. Use it however you want.

Work with us.

We're a small team that hires slowly and deliberately. If you read this far, that probably means something.
