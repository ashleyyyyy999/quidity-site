Frequently asked questions

1

What is a unified sales inbox?

A unified sales inbox brings all your sales conversations across channels into one place. It gives your teams a single view to manage replies, track outreach, and stay organized without switching tools. Quidity’s unified sales inbox, Unibox, is used by teams like Clara and Deel to centralize replies.

2

How can your teams reply faster to emails?

Sales teams reply faster by using AI-generated reply drafts and working from a unified inbox. This reduces context switching and helps reps respond quickly with relevant, personalized messages.

3

Can Unibox handle multiple mailboxes and channels?

Yes. Quidity’s Unibox consolidates conversations across multiple mailboxes and channels such as email and social, allowing your teams to manage outreach and replies from one place.

4

How does Unibox help teams stay organized?

Quidity’s Unibox combines the inbox and outbox to help teams stay organized. It lets them filter conversations, track scheduled messages, mark replies as read or unread, and focus on high-intent conversations.

5

Who is Unibox best for?

Unibox is best for builders, SDRs, and sales managers who handle high volumes of outreach and need a faster, more organized way to manage sales conversations.
