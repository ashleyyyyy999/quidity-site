Today’s buyers move fast. They switch channels, drop signals, and expect relevant conversations at every step.

But most sales tools still ask reps to do the hard work manually, digging for leads, chasing stale data, and juggling outreach.

Amplemarket is different.

We're building the future of sales, a foundation for AI agents that work like teammates, spotting buying signals, researching prospects in-depth, and delivering timely outreach all in one place.

Read our manifesto

Ten or fifteen years ago, that's really when we started seeing some of the concepts that sales teams still use today really be pioneered. The way I think about sales, it's I think it's a problem that combines, at the same time, a lot of, like, emotional intelligence and data intelligence. Sales is ultimately about trust. Oftentimes, you think a company will buy from a different company, and companies are these abstract entities for most people, but I think people buy from people in the end. And I really care a lot about the human aspect of sales. And that's why one of our taglines is Human + AI. AI is gonna help sales teams to be ten times, if not more productive. It's going to be almost like this coworker that is working alongside you to help you with all your flows in the day to day. I don't believe that AI is going to replace sales teams at all. It's gonna make them way more efficient and way more productive. Our mission at Amplemarket is to help companies grow. Amplemarket is an all in one platform in the sense that it can do end to end workflows within the platform. You can use it to find and then connect with your next customer without needing three or four different platforms to do so. I want to make it easier for two humans to connect. One of the humans is experiencing a big problem that they want to solve within their business, the other human has a potential solution, and I want them to connect. We believe that the problem that we're solving is quite an important one. An SDR or an AE, every single morning getting, like, notifications from agents that have done overnight research on their accounts. If you do things well, people should be spending more time with people and less time on what I call crunch monkey tasks where they're just cranking a wheel and doing the same thing over and over again that where you don't add any value. And in the end, we think that this technological shift that we're going through right now is going to yield incredible opportunities that is gonna make it possible to really build a generational company that changes how go to market tooling works and what's possible, and we wanna be that company.
