Questions you may have

Frequently asked questions

What is Mailbox Recommendation?

Mailbox Recommendation utilizes Quidity's weekly automated Inbox Placement Tests to promptly identify and choose healthy mailboxes (those capable of landing in your prospect's inbox) for your cold outreach campaigns during sequence building.

How can I recover unhealthy/spam-flagged mailboxes?

Quidity recommends that users temporarily pause flagged mailboxes and refrain from adding new leads to them. Enabling the Deliverability Booster feature aids in restoring the reputation of flagged mailboxes, making them suitable for reactivation once they pass Inbox Placement Tests.
